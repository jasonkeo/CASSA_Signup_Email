Welcome!

On behalf of the Computing and Security Student Association (CASSA), we would like to extend a warm welcome to you! We are thrilled to have you join our community of passionate individuals who share a common interest in all computing and security.

As a CASSA member, you can take advantage of a range of CASSA resources, such as:

  • Engaging with your computing and security cohort online through our Discord
  • Establishing connections with fellow students and industry professionals at our in-person events
  • Improving your skills through hands-on workshops
  • Receiving timely infomation about relevant university and industry opportunities

As a new member, you can expect occasional emails for important updates. Otherwise, our primary communication platform is Discord, where you can engage in real-time discussions, share resources and stay informed about club activities.

In the meantime, feel free to explore our website and follow our social media channels Instagram, Facebook and LinkedIn. If you're on campus, one of our friendly team members is likely to be found at the CASSA helpdesk, located at JO18.209.

If you have any questions or ideas you'd like to share, dont hesitate to reach out to us at committee@cassa.au

We look forward to sharing a fulfilling a university experience together.

Kind regards,
CASSA committee
